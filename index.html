Why "DialektoSupra"?

Dialektos (Greek): dia- (through) + lektos (selected/spoken). The word through which we express ourselves—language, idiom, discourse.

Supra (Latin): Above, beyond, transcending.

Together: A space that discusses the darknet transcending language barriers. The inverted word order? A subtle nod to the irreverence inherent in the culture we explore.
